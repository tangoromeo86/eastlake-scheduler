How to use the schedule viewer

Views

☰
Games

Full list of games for the selected division. Use the team filter to see only your team's games. Hit ↓ Export CSV to download a spreadsheet.

👥
Teams

Each team's schedule as a card — opponent, date, time, home/away, and field. Tap a team's Export button to download their schedule as a CSV.

📅
Calendar

Month-by-month view. Choose a specific team to see their games highlighted, or pick All teams to see every game in the division at a glance.

🏛
Fields

All games grouped by date and field. Use the field filter to zoom in on a specific venue, and export a field schedule as CSV.

Requesting a change

✉
Request Change button

Click Request Change next to any game in the Games view to open a guided form. It will draft an email to the league admin with all the details pre-filled — just hit Send.

Divisions

📄
Division tabs

Use the tabs at the top to switch between divisions. Each division has its own schedule, team list, and calendar.
